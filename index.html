My Story

Hi! My name is Edgar, and I have a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. Since early 2020, I've been working in the web development industry for + years now. I built several websites but only included a few in my website portfolio.

I graduated from college in 2013 as an IT student. I attempted to pursue a programming career immediately after graduation and even received an interview invitation. Unfortunately, I was unable to do so at the time. A variety of factors, including the environment, and the cost of my trip to Manila. That was understandable given my humble upbringing.

As a result, my first job was as a cashier at a gas station three towns away from home. I work 12 hour shifts six days a week and 24 hour shifts twice a month. I was 19 at the time and said yes to everything; things were not easy and it is not a fulfilling job either. I moved from one job to another that was not in the technology industry.

The Covid 19 pandemic struck hard in early 2020, and I was laid off as a result. In my spare time, I attempted to re-learn web development, and it came naturally to me. I freelance using my current skills to keep myself afloat while looking for a web developer job.

If you are looking for a Frontend Developer, please let me know how I can assist, and I hope you would then join me on my journey.
